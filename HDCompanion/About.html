Our Mission

HelpDesk Companion was born out of a simple observation: Support technicians spend too much time switching between multiple management consoles. By consolidating Active Directory, Remote Workstation tools, and Service Desk asset data into a single, cohesive dashboard, we empower our team to resolve issues faster and with higher accuracy.

🚀

Boosting Efficiency

Reducing ticket resolution time by leveraging the most frequently used support tools.

Key Capabilities

Automated AD Monitoring

Real-time detection and optional auto-unlocking of locked accounts across the domain.

One-Click Remoting

Seamlessly manage printers, processes, and sessions without interrupting the user.

Data Integrity

Centralized logging ensures a complete audit trail of all management actions performed.
